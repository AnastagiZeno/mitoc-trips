What do I need to do to go on trips?

You must have up-to-date personal information, and have attended both lectures.

How do I sign up for a trip?

Leaders will post trips, along with a brief description to list of trips. Signups for each trip will open at a time given in the "sign up" section of the trip summary.

When signups open, you can sign up with the form that appears below the trip summary. You may only sign up once.

How do I know if I'm on the trip?

If the trip was a first-come, first-serve trip, you will immediately be able to see if you made the participant list, or have been placed in the waiting list. If the trip is a lottery trip, you will know once the lottery algorithm has run. For more information, see the lottery section.

What timezone are trip times in?

All times are in Eastern Standard Time.
