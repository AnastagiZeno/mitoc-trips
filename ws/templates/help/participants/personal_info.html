Why do you need my personal information?

It the event of a medical emergency, it is essential that your trip leaders know your medical history. If a trip were to be reported missing, a description of involved vehicles would be given to search and rescue teams.

Who can view this information?

Members of the Winter Safety Committee are able to see this information, as well as your trip leaders. No other participants can see this information.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. If any aspect of your medical history pertains to a trip you're signed up for, a trip leader will approach you to discuss the issue in private.
